Arnaud Klipfel

Welcome! I am a Robotics Ph.D. student in the Institute for Robotics and Intelligent Machines and the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Georgia Institute of Technology. I am working under the supervision of Dr. Seth Hutchinson co-advised by Dr. Sehoon Ha. I received a Master of Sceience in the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering at The Georgia Institute of Technology in December 2022. My Master Thesis work investigated Deep Reinforcement Learning (DRL) for quadrupedal locomotion to generate more natural and smoother gaits. I also earned a French Engineering Degree (Diplôme d'Ingénieur) from ENSTA Bretagne in mobile robotics.
